Who is Ivan Jara-Marquez now?

Ivan Jara-Marquez is currently a sophomore in Kirkland House at Harvard University concentrating in Neuroscience. He isn't quite sure what he wants to do in his life quite yet, but he'll get there. After this course, he might even choose to pursue a secondary in CS. He thinks it's pretty fun. In short, the future knows no bounds for Ivan.

Where did he come from?

Ivan was born on August 3rd, 2001 to Francisco and Claudia Jara. He hails from Thousand Palms in the Coachella Valley, California. He's lived there all his life and comes from a Mexican family. He has three siblings: two twin brothers and one sister, all of whom are younger than him (fun fact: his sister has the same birthday as him).

How was his childhood?

Ivan grew up as a happy kid. He went to a nice small elementary school in Thousand Palms where he excelled in his classes. He began to practice karate at the age of five and stuck with it for most of his younger years. As a young kid, he enjoyed playing video games and spending time with his cousins whenever he could. After elementary school he moved on to attend the middle school that his mom went to when she was his age. At this point he wanted to branch out and joined the soccer and basketball teams (sports that he was very bad at). How was High School?

After middle school, Ivan went on to attend Palm Desert High School in Palm Desert, CA. It was here that Ivan really began to shine! He really came out of his shell by joining several clubs such as Young Life, Youth and Government, his local government's Youth Advisory Council, and more. This is also where he met the majority of his closest friends, people who he still values to this day. High school was certainly an important development for Ivan.
